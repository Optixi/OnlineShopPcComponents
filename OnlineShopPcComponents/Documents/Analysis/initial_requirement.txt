Online Shop Pc Components -

       Java Web Application used to sell pc components .

    ● One window for CRUD operations on Products ( product id , specification , price , manufacturer):  allows the viewing of all the products , adding a new product , deleting an existing product and updating an existing product .

    ● One window for CRUD operations on Orders(order id , order date , price of order , status of order) allow of the viewing of all the orders , adding a new order , deleting an existing order and updating an existing order)

    ● One window for CRUD operations on Basket (products ,  quantity , total price ) allows the viewing of all the products in the basket , updating  quantity of a product , viewing the total price cost for the products)

    ● One window for CRUD operations on Profile ( name , lastname , email , address ) allows the viewing of all the profiles , adding a new profile , deleting an existing profile , updating an existing profile .

    ● One window for client:
      -view all products, filter products, add products to cart -make orders,-make CRUD operations on basket and profile.

    ● One window for register , login and forgot password .
